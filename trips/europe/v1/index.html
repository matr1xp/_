European Winter Adventure: UK, France, and Italy

Experience the magic of winter in Europe with our 18 days adventure across United Kingdom, France, Italy. From Christmas markets to historic landmarks, this itinerary is designed for Family of 4 (2 adults, 2 teenagers) to enjoy the best of European winter.

Practical Information

Transportation Details

London

Options: Underground (Tube), Buses, Taxis, Uber

Recommended Pass: Oyster card or contactless payment

Cost: £15-20 per day per person for unlimited travel

Tips: Avoid rush hour (8-9:30 AM and 5-6:30 PM) if possible

Paris

Options: Metro, Buses, RER (suburban trains), Taxis

Recommended Pass: Paris Visite pass for tourists

Cost: €12-15 per day per person for unlimited travel

Tips: Metro is the fastest way to get around the city

Milan

Options: Metro, Trams, Buses, Taxis

Recommended Pass: 24/48/72-hour urban pass

Cost: €7-15 per day per person depending on pass type

Tips: The Metro is efficient and connects most tourist areas

Venice

Options: Vaporetto (water bus), Walking, Water taxis

Recommended Pass: ACTV Tourist Travel Card

Cost: €25-40 per person for multi-day pass

Tips: Venice is best explored on foot, but vaporetto is useful for longer distances

Florence

Options: Walking, Buses, Taxis

Recommended Pass: Not necessary as city center is walkable

Cost: €1.50 per bus ticket

Tips: Most attractions are within walking distance in the historic center

Rome

Options: Metro, Buses, Trams, Taxis

Recommended Pass: Roma Pass (includes transportation and museums)

Cost: €10-15 per day per person for unlimited travel

Tips: The Metro is limited but useful for major attractions; buses cover more areas

Winter Packing Tips

Clothing

  • Warm winter coats
  • Layers (thermal underwear, sweaters, cardigans)
  • Waterproof jackets
  • Comfortable walking shoes (waterproof if possible)
  • Warm socks
  • Gloves, scarves, and hats
  • One nice outfit for special dinners or events

Accessories

  • Universal power adapter
  • Portable phone charger
  • Day backpack
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Umbrella or rain poncho
  • Camera

Documents

  • Passports
  • Travel insurance documents
  • Printed hotel reservations
  • Train tickets or confirmations
  • Credit cards and some cash in local currency

Winter Specific

  • Hand warmers
  • Lip balm and moisturizer (cold weather can be drying)
  • Sunglasses (winter sun can be bright, especially with snow)
  • Tissues for cold weather sniffles
