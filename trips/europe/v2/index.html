Transportation

Flights

Arrival Flight

Fly into Rome Fiumicino Airport (FCO) on December 15.

Transfer options from airport to city center:

  • Leonardo Express Train: Direct to Roma Termini (30 minutes)
  • Terravision Bus: Budget option to city center (45-60 minutes)
  • Taxi: Fixed fare of €48 to central Rome

Return Options

Depart from London Heathrow (LHR) or London Gatwick (LGW) after December 31.

Transfer options to London airports:

  • Heathrow Express: Fast train from Paddington Station
  • Tube: Piccadilly Line to Heathrow (more affordable)
  • Gatwick Express: From Victoria Station to Gatwick

Rome to Paris (Dec 20-21)

Train Options

  • Providers: Italo, TGV INOUI, and Frecciarossa services
  • Travel Time: 10-11 hours
  • Price Range: €75 - €290
  • Features: High-speed and night train options available
  • Note: Seat reservations are mandatory
  • Scenery: Beautiful Alpine views along the route

Flight Options

  • Providers: Ryanair and other airlines
  • Travel Time: Approximately 5 hours 15 minutes (including airport procedures)
  • Price Range: €40 - €220
  • Budget Option: Ryanair flights from €21 when booked in advance

Bus Options

  • Provider: FlixBus
  • Price Range: $92.98 - $97.99
  • Schedule: Multiple daily departures (12:10 AM to 11:50 PM)
  • Duration: 20+ hours

Travel Tips

  • Book transportation 2-3 months in advance for best rates
  • Consider total travel costs including transfers to/from stations/airports
  • For our group of 9 people, look into group discounts on trains
  • Fastest option: Flying (5h 15m including airport procedures)
  • Most scenic option: Train journey through the Alps
  • Most budget-friendly: Depends on advance booking and promotions

Paris to London (Dec 26-27)

Eurostar Train (Recommended)

  • Travel Time: Approximately 2 hours 16-20 minutes
  • Price Range: €44 to $370
  • Average Prices: $227 (30 days in advance), $132 (7 days in advance)
  • Departure: Paris Gare du Nord station
  • Arrival: London St. Pancras International
  • Benefits: City center to city center, no airport hassles

Alternative Options

  • Bus (FlixBus): 7 hours 5 minutes, starting from $24.49 (8 rides daily)
  • Flight: 3-4 hours including airport procedures
  • Car Ferry: Longer journey but scenic option

Travel Tips

  • Eurostar is most recommended for speed and convenience
  • Book tickets in advance for better pricing
  • For our group of 4 people, consider family or group seating options
  • Check luggage allowances before booking
  • Consider flexible tickets for potential weather disruptions in December
  • Use comparison sites like Omio or Trainline to find the best deals

Local Transit

Money-Saving Tips

  • Rome: Purchase a 7-day transport pass
  • Paris: Consider Paris Visite pass for unlimited travel
  • London: Use Oyster card with daily fare caps

Group Travel Tips

  • Travel during off-peak hours when possible
  • Consider group tickets where available
  • Use mobile apps for real-time transit information
  • Allow extra time when traveling with mixed age groups
