Transportation

Flights

Arrival Flight

Fly into Rome Fiumicino Airport (FCO) on December 15.

Transfer options from airport to city center:

  • Leonardo Express Train: Direct to Roma Termini (30 minutes)
  • Terravision Bus: Budget option to city center (45-60 minutes)
  • Taxi: Fixed fare of €48 to central Rome

Return Options

Depart from London Heathrow (LHR) or London Gatwick (LGW) after December 31.

Transfer options to London airports:

  • Heathrow Express: Fast train from Paddington Station
  • Tube: Piccadilly Line to Heathrow (more affordable)
  • Gatwick Express: From Victoria Station to Gatwick

Rome to Venice (Dec 18)

Train Options

  • Providers: Italo and Frecciarossa high-speed trains
  • Travel Time: 3.5-4 hours
  • Price Range: €35 - €120
  • Features: Direct high-speed service
  • Note: Seat reservations are mandatory

Travel Tips

  • Book tickets in advance for best rates
  • Trains depart from Roma Termini
  • Arrive at Venezia Santa Lucia
  • Consider morning departure to maximize time in Venice

Venice to Milan (Dec 20)

Train Options

  • Providers: Italo and Frecciarossa high-speed trains
  • Travel Time: 2.5 hours
  • Price Range: €30 - €100
  • Features: Direct high-speed service
  • Note: Seat reservations are mandatory

Travel Tips

  • Book tickets in advance for best rates
  • Trains depart from Venezia Santa Lucia
  • Arrive at Milano Centrale
  • Consider afternoon departure to maximize time in Venice

Milan to Paris (Dec 23)

Train Options

  • Providers: TGV INOUI and Frecciarossa services
  • Travel Time: 7-8 hours
  • Price Range: €75 - €290
  • Features: High-speed and night train options available
  • Note: Seat reservations are mandatory
  • Scenery: Beautiful Alpine views along the route

Flight Options

  • Providers: Various airlines
  • Travel Time: Approximately 4 hours (including airport procedures)
  • Price Range: €40 - €220
  • Budget Option: Low-cost carriers from €40 when booked in advance

Travel Tips

  • Book transportation 2-3 months in advance for best rates
  • Consider total travel costs including transfers to/from stations/airports
  • Fastest option: Flying (4 hours including airport procedures)
  • Most scenic option: Train journey through the Alps
  • Most budget-friendly: Depends on advance booking and promotions

Paris to London (Dec 26-27)

Eurostar Train (Recommended)

  • Travel Time: Approximately 2 hours 16-20 minutes
  • Price Range: €44 to $370
  • Average Prices: $227 (30 days in advance), $132 (7 days in advance)
  • Departure: Paris Gare du Nord station
  • Arrival: London St. Pancras International
  • Benefits: City center to city center, no airport hassles

Alternative Options

  • Bus (FlixBus): 7 hours 5 minutes, starting from $24.49 (8 rides daily)
  • Flight: 3-4 hours including airport procedures
  • Car Ferry: Longer journey but scenic option

Travel Tips

  • Eurostar is most recommended for speed and convenience
  • Book tickets in advance for better pricing
  • For our group of 4 people, consider family or group seating options
  • Check luggage allowances before booking
  • Consider flexible tickets for potential weather disruptions in December
  • Use comparison sites like Omio or Trainline to find the best deals

Local Transit

Milan (ATM Network)

  • Metro System:
    • 4 lines covering the city center and suburbs
    • Runs from 6:00 AM to midnight
    • Single ticket: €2.20 (valid for 90 minutes)
    • 24-hour pass: €7.60
    • 3-day tourist ticket: €13.00
  • Trams & Buses:
    • Extensive network complementing metro
    • Same tickets valid across all modes
    • Night buses replace metro after midnight

Venice (ACTV Network)

  • Vaporetto (Water Bus):
    • Main transportation in Venice
    • Line 1: Grand Canal route (most scenic)
    • Lines 4.1/4.2, 5.1/5.2: Circular routes
    • Line 12: Service to Murano, Burano, and Torcello
  • Tickets & Passes:
    • Single ticket: €9.50 (75 minutes)
    • 24-hour pass: €25.00
    • 48-hour pass: €35.00
    • 72-hour pass: €45.00
    • 7-day pass: €65.00
  • Important Notes:
    • Always validate tickets before boarding
    • Keep tickets until end of journey
    • Consider multi-day passes for cost savings
    • Download ACTV app for real-time schedules

Money-Saving Tips

  • Milan: Purchase multi-day passes for unlimited travel
  • Venice: Get tourist travel cards for extended stays
  • Rome: Use 7-day transport pass
  • Paris: Consider Paris Visite pass
  • London: Use Oyster card with daily caps

Group Travel Tips

  • Travel during off-peak hours when possible
  • Consider group tickets where available
  • Use mobile apps for real-time transit information
  • Allow extra time when traveling with mixed age groups
  • In Venice, consider private water taxi for groups of 4+
