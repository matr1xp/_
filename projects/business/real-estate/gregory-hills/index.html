Why Invest in Gregory Hills?

Discover the potential of this rapidly growing suburb in Sydney's South West

Gregory Hills is a rapidly growing suburb located in the Camden Council area of New South Wales, Australia. With a population of approximately 9,142 residents and a significant growth rate of 215.5% between 2016-2021, this suburb presents compelling opportunities for property investors looking to buy, renovate, and sell for profit.

Our comprehensive investment guide provides you with all the information you need to make informed decisions about real estate investing in Gregory Hills, from market analysis to step-by-step strategies and financial considerations.

Market Insights

Gregory Hills has shown consistent growth over the past few years, with property values increasing by 8.3% annually. The suburb is particularly attractive to young families, with the predominant age group being 30-39 years old.

With ongoing infrastructure developments including the Gregory Hills Corporate Park and improved transportation links, the area is projected to continue its growth trajectory, making it an ideal location for property investment.
