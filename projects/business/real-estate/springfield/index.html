Why Invest in Springfield?

A rapidly growing region with strong investment potential

Springfield, Queensland is one of Australia's fastest growing regions, offering exceptional opportunities for property investors. Located in South East Queensland, this master-planned community has seen significant development and infrastructure investment, making it an attractive option for both homebuyers and investors.

With a strong population growth rate of 6.0% per annum (more than 4 times the State and National averages), Springfield is experiencing rapid expansion and development. This growth is supported by substantial infrastructure investment, including the $6 billion expansion of Springfield Central Precinct.

Investment Strategies

Approaches to maximize your Springfield property investment

Investment Approaches

Whether you're focusing on rental yield or capital growth, Springfield offers strong potential for both investment strategies. With rental yields between 3.9% and 4.5%, investors can achieve solid cash flow while benefiting from the area's impressive capital growth.

Key Investment Considerations:

  • Houses currently yield 3.9% to 4.43% rental returns
  • Units in Springfield Lakes yield around 4.52%
  • Strong tenant demand due to rapid growth and good amenities
  • Diverse property options across different price points

Tax Considerations

Understanding the tax implications of property investment

Capital Gains Tax

From January 1, 2025, significant changes to the Capital Gains Tax will take effect, including a reduction in the CGT discount from 50% to 25%.

Negative Gearing

Negative gearing remains a viable strategy for property investors, allowing you to offset rental property losses against other income sources.

Depreciation Benefits

Property investors can claim deductions for the decline in value of a property and its assets, with newer properties offering more substantial benefits.
