Demographics of Car Owners

Understanding the car ownership patterns in Sydney provides valuable insights for targeting potential customers:

  • 43.6% of Sydney households own one car
  • 10.5% of households own two cars
  • 2.0% of households own three or more cars
  • 46% of Greater Sydney households have access to two or more motor vehicles (compared to 53% nationally)
  • The average number of motor vehicles per household in Sydney is 1.8
  • 673,969 households in Australia do not have a motor vehicle, primarily among lone households
