Regulatory Requirements

Complete guide to licenses, permits, insurance, and compliance requirements for operating a mobile auto-repair business in Sydney, NSW.

Regulatory Requirements

Business Registration Requirements

Australian Business Number (ABN)

  • Description: Essential first step for business registration
  • Issuing Authority: Australian Business Register
  • Application Process: Apply online through the Australian Business Register
  • Cost: Free
  • Renewal: Not required

Business Name Registration

  • Description: Required if operating under a name other than your personal name
  • Issuing Authority: Australian Securities and Investments Commission (ASIC)
  • Application Process: Register through ASIC Connect
  • Cost: $37 for 1 year or $88 for 3 years (as of 2025)
  • Renewal: Every 1 or 3 years

Goods and Services Tax (GST) Registration

  • Description: Required if annual turnover exceeds $75,000
  • Issuing Authority: Australian Taxation Office (ATO)
  • Application Process: Register through ATO Business Portal
  • Cost: Free
  • Renewal: Not required, but must be maintained

Trade Licenses and Certifications

Motor Vehicle Repairer Licence

  • Description: Mandatory for self-employed motor vehicle repairers or repair business owners in NSW
  • Issuing Authority: NSW Fair Trading through Service NSW
  • Application Process: Apply online through Service NSW
  • Cost: Varies based on duration (1-year, 3-year, or 5-year terms)
  • Renewal: Required upon expiration
  • Compliance Requirements: All employees must be qualified and certified in their specific repair work classes

Motor Vehicle Tradesperson Certificate

  • Description: Mandatory for anyone working on a vehicle's mechanical operation or structure
  • Issuing Authority: NSW Fair Trading
  • Application Process: Apply through NSW Fair Trading
  • Cost: Varies based on certification type
  • Renewal: Check with NSW Fair Trading for renewal requirements
  • Required Qualifications:
    • Certificate III in Automotive Studies, or
    • Certificate IV in Automotive Mechanical Overhauling, or
    • Certificate III in Automotive Body Repair
    • Completion of a 4-year apprenticeship is common

Mobile Business Permits

Mobile Workshop Operation Permit

  • Description: May be required depending on local council regulations
  • Issuing Authority: Local Sydney council where business operates
  • Application Process: Contact specific local council
  • Cost: Varies by council
  • Renewal: Typically annual
  • Compliance Requirements: Must adhere to local zoning and operational restrictions

Trade Waste Permit

  • Description: Required for businesses generating automotive waste
  • Issuing Authority: Sydney Water or local water authority
  • Application Process: Apply through local water authority
  • Cost: Varies based on business size and waste volume
  • Renewal: Typically annual
  • Compliance Requirements: Proper disposal of automotive fluids and waste

Insurance Requirements

Public Liability Insurance

  • Description: Critical for all mobile auto repair businesses
  • Coverage: Protects against third-party claims for property damage or personal injury
  • Recommended Coverage: Minimum $10-20 million
  • Cost: Varies based on business size, services offered, and coverage amount
  • Providers: Upcover, Trade Risk, Ausure OnCover, Marsh Australia
  • Renewal: Typically annual

Tools and Equipment Insurance

  • Description: Covers theft or damage to professional tools and equipment
  • Coverage: Protection for expensive tools and equipment used in mobile operations
  • Recommended Coverage: Based on value of tools and equipment
  • Cost: Varies based on value of items covered
  • Providers: Upcover, Trade Risk, Ausure OnCover, Marsh Australia
  • Renewal: Typically annual

Commercial Motor Insurance

  • Description: Covers work vehicles used in the business
  • Coverage: Protects against damage, theft, and potential liability while using work vehicles
  • Recommended Coverage: Comprehensive coverage recommended
  • Cost: Varies based on vehicle value and business operations
  • Providers: Upcover, Trade Risk, Ausure OnCover, Marsh Australia, major auto insurers
  • Renewal: Typically annual

Products Liability Insurance

  • Description: Protects against claims arising from repair work or parts used
  • Coverage: Covers potential issues resulting from services performed
  • Recommended Coverage: Based on business size and services offered
  • Cost: Varies based on coverage amount and business operations
  • Providers: Upcover, Trade Risk, Ausure OnCover, Marsh Australia
  • Renewal: Typically annual

Environmental Regulations

Waste Management Requirements

  • Governing Legislation: Protection of the Environment Operations (Waste) Regulation 2014
  • Regulatory Authority: NSW Environment Protection Authority (EPA)
  • Compliance Requirements:
    • Proper handling, storing, transporting, processing, recycling, and disposing of automotive waste
    • Special care for hazardous materials like automotive fluids, dangerous goods, and chemical waste
    • Use of certified recycling facilities for vehicle parts disposal
    • Adherence to EPA NSW guidelines

Hazardous Waste Handling

  • Description: Special requirements for handling automotive fluids, oils, and other hazardous materials
  • Regulatory Authority: NSW EPA
  • Compliance Requirements:
    • Proper storage containers for different types of waste
    • Segregation of incompatible wastes
    • Secure storage areas to prevent spills and leaks
    • Proper labeling of all hazardous materials
    • Record-keeping of waste generation and disposal

Waste Transport and Disposal

  • Description: Requirements for transporting and disposing of automotive waste
  • Regulatory Authority: NSW EPA
  • Compliance Requirements:
    • Use of licensed waste transporters
    • Proper documentation for waste transport (waste tracking forms)
    • Disposal only at licensed facilities
    • Maintenance of waste disposal records

Workplace Health and Safety Requirements

  • Governing Legislation: Work Health and Safety Act and Regulations
  • Regulatory Authority: SafeWork NSW
  • Compliance Requirements:
    • Development of workplace safety policies and procedures
    • Regular risk assessments
    • Provision of appropriate Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
    • Safety training for all workers
    • Incident reporting procedures
